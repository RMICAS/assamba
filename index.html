Why Choose Us?

Experience and Growth:

With over 5 years of experience, ASSAMBA has cultivated over 2,000 bamboo plants on a 3-hectare farm and operates a nursery producing 2,000 seedlings every 6 months.

Community Empowerment:

Our training programs have equipped 40 farmers with skills in bamboo cultivation and biochar production.

Expanding Impact:

Backed by a €15,000 PACJA grant, we are expanding 10 hectares of bamboo crops to foster sustainability.
